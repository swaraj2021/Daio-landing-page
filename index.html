Main Points of Go-to-Market Strategy

Strategic approach to market entry and growth

Main Goal

The goal is gain awareness and procure the unsatisfied DIY investors from the investments app with viral growth strategies.

Target Audience

Primarily TA segments:

Gen Z and millennial (25-40 y.o) and Gen X (40-50 y.o) DIY investors who lost money or didn't earn as much as desired on competitor investment apps. This audience is identified as most likely to try new investment apps like "DAIO" and become early adopters. The target market is the US, specifically cities and suburbs.

Secondarily:

25-50 y.o with no experience in investing.

Early Stage Market Penetration

Target of 0.5% to 3% of 21 million people (Serviceable Obtainable Market - SOM), which translates to 105,000 to 630,000 people, within 0-3 years.

Early Market Penetration Timeline

1. Launch & Initial Adoption (0-6 months):

Activities: Beta testing, early user acquisition, heavy marketing (referrals, influencers, partnerships).

Goal: Reach 0.5%-1% penetration.

2. Growth & Validation (6-18 months):

Activities: Scaling user base, refining product-market fit, adjusting pricing, improving UX, handling compliance.

Goal: Reach 1%-3% penetration.

3. Transition to Scaling (18-36 months):

Activities: Brand awareness increases, organic adoption grows, expansion beyond early adopters.

Goal: Exceed 3% penetration and move into the scaling phase.
